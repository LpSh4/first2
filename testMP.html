Big steps in Russian language learning

Russian language is certainly very hard and difficult language to learn. Based on that, we, Russian students from Architecture University, decided to conduct a test for foreign students with desire of learning that intricate language. This test has been tested by a couple of students from different countries, and majestically called "Very hard or even impossible to perform". This test was hosted by a proffessional with extensive experiense of working with international students, so there is nothing to be scared of. Make sure that you know, at least, basic and middle-difficulty rules of that language before you start.

Check it!

Learn from your mistakes!

As you may know, the best way to improve knowledge about anything is practice, and based on that, we made this test as hard (for novice native speakers, of course) as possible. In this test, to to prevent further ignorance and mistakes in that complex sentence and word constructions we will give you a little tips, and if you will suddenly make a mistake, this test will tell you what caused that mistake and how to facilitate future use of text structures.
